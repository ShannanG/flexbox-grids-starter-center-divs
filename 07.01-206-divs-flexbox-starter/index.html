I was born in Herts and raised in Devon. I love the feel of the sand beneath my feet and the breath-taking moorland and coastal views, but I can't resist heading to the city every now and then.
My passion: I LOVE textiles so I'm a needlework pattern designer on the side. You may've seen my patterns published in sewing magazines the world over.
A fun fact: I get a kick out of repurposing vintage thrifted treasures! I think my ability to take something that already exists and improve it serves me well as a dev.
